Conceptual Foundation

Pretty Coinz is an independent design study evaluating how miniature works of art hold a distinct appeal based entirely on visual merit.

This archive is a follow-up to my automated savings framework and mini-doc featured in a New York Times national ad campaign. Operating quietly in the background, this system frees up time for artistic pursuits. For responsible analysis, the study operates within a strictly bounded creative allowance, keeping my broader finances entirely private.

Curatorial Methodology

The collection’s final anchor, the Beal Mountain Silver Proof Round, will be acquired upon completing a long-term personal endeavor. I use physical, aesthetic tokens to quietly catalog my personal benchmarks, designating this specific specimen as the final milestone marker purely for its exceptional design and physical composition.

This future anchor will sit alongside an eclectic spectrum of visual items—including pop-culture icons like Darth Vader, intricate floral art coins, shimmering gold banknotes, artistic Goldbacks, NASA Artemis rounds, and precision die-cast auction cars—forming a unique study centered purely on secondary-market "Eye Appeal."
