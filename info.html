Conceptual Foundation

Pretty Coinz is an independent design study evaluating how miniature works of art hold a distinct appeal based entirely on visual merit.

This personal archive reflects a lifestyle philosophy centered on automated savings—a framework for which I was individually profiled by the New York Times for a national campaign. The system operates quietly in the background, freeing up personal time to pursue artistic and archival passions.

To maintain strict creative focus, this study operates within a strictly bounded creative allowance for pure curation, keeping my broader personal finances entirely private.

Curatorial Methodology

The collection’s final anchor is the Beal Mountain Silver Proof Round, chosen to mark the completion of a long-term personal timeline. By utilizing physical, aesthetic artifacts to quietly catalog personal benchmarks, this specific specimen was selected purely for its exceptional design and physical composition.

This anchor sits alongside an eclectic spectrum of visual items—including pop-culture icons like Darth Vader, intricate floral art coins, shimmering gold banknotes, artistic Goldbacks, NASA Artemis rounds, and precision die-cast auction cars—forming a unique study centered purely on secondary-market "Eye Appeal."
