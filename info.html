PRETTY COINZ // THE BEAUTY INDEX

🏛️ The Beauty Index
Institutional Foundation
Pretty Coinz is a numismatic study inspired by PCGS analysis of artistic merit. This experiment evaluates the capacity for aesthetic assets to retain value or surpass traditional market benchmarks. Supported by a disciplined investment philosophy I was featured in a The New York Times ad campaign, and have established a portfolio that affords the freedom to pursue specialized research.
Active Research Methodology
To visualize this trajectory, I acquire a specific coin for every Capital Milestone reached—serving as Physical Milestone Trackers for my invested equity growth. Beyond these benchmarks, I maintain a Qualitative Control Group: non-milestone specimens subjected to Comparative Aesthetic Analysis to illustrate the variables within the Beauty Index and test the limits of visual merit.
🏛️ The Basal Reserve
Foundational Equity // Decoupled
Beneath the active trajectory lies the Basal Reserve: my direct real estate holdings tracked via the personal iltravjer (Individual Land-Trace Real Asset Value Joint-Equity Register). Represented by 24k gold, this foundational asset remains decoupled from my active milestones to maintain the mathematical integrity of the study.
🏛️ The Sovereign Anchor
Physical Milestone Signaling
This project concludes with the Beal Benchmark: an inquiry spanning over a decade aimed at independently surpassing the valuation of the official Beal Prize. The archive’s first chapter will close with the acquisition of the Beal Mountain Proof. This anchor closes the loop between the Archivist’s award-winning mathematical theory on the Beal Conjecture (Westfair Milli Award) and physical reality.
